UR3 Screw is a sample URCaps project. Use with care!


UR3 Screw rotates Wrist 3 until either a given rotations, torque,
time or distance is reached.
Note that the torque tolerance on a UR3 is +/- 5 Nm.


 
Choose direction:
Clockwise
Counter-clockwise

Choose desired options:

Rotations: revolutions
Torque: Nm
Distance: mm
Timeout: seconds
 

General parameters:

Force: N
Rotation speed: °/sec
